PYTHON PROGRAMMING WORKSHOP

A Brief report on WORKSHOP ON PYTHON PROGRAMMING Held during 9th and 16th October 2018 Organised by: Students of Dept of Computer Science,PESCE,Mandya This Workshop was organised for students of B.E 3rd and 5th Sem. Aiming to provide an intense training on coding using python for UG students, two days workshop on 'PYTHON PROGRAMMING' was held on 9th and 16th of October 2018 at Department of Computer Science,PESCE,Mandya.

On Day - 1 i.e., on 9th October 2018, Nanda Kumar V hosted a survey for the participants to download the essential software for python pragramming. Pratik R Modi guided the participants through the installation process of these softwares. After installation of softwares on participant's laptops. Varun Achar gave a brief introduction on "Why Pyhton programming ?" to the participants. Sunad K and Sanjay N S trained the participants on basics of python programming such as • Variables • Data types • Printing and Reading values • Branching and Looping • Functions along with some examples and made the participants write their own code and made them execute on their own laptops. Some quiz question and simple application problems were given to the participants to make them more familiar with the language.

Day 2 i.e., on 16th October 2018, The participants were taught classes and object concept and matplotlib libraries used to plot a graph using raw data by Sammith B S and Varun Achar The workshop was then concluded by giving certificates to the particioants fron the hands of Dr.Vinay S, Prof, Dept of Comuter Science, PESCE, Mandya. This workshop was basically designed to give a intense training on Python Programming for the students of 3rd and 5th sem. Around 35 students participitated in the workshop. This workshop was appriciated by all the participants as they were able to add another programming language to their technical skill set. Varun Achar, Sunad K, Nanda Kumar V, Sanjay N S, Pratik R Modi, Sammith B S, Students of 5th sem, Dept of Computer Science, PESCE, Mandya convened and organised this event jointly with Dept of Computer Science, PESCE, Mandya.

About Us

The Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) program was started in the year 1984. The main focus of the department is to produce graduates & post graduates with strong fundamentals in Computer Science and Engineering domain. The course is an outcome based structured to help students to achieve this goal. The course was accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA).
